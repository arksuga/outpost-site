Outpost

Outpost is a web application that allows users to log in with TikTok and publish videos to their own TikTok accounts after explicit authorization.

What Outpost Does

  • Secure TikTok login integration
  • Video upload and management
  • Direct publishing to your own TikTok account

User Authorization

Outpost only performs actions explicitly requested and authorized by the user. No content is posted without user consent.
