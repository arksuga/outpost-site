Outpost

Outpost is a web application that allows users to log in with TikTok and publish videos to their own TikTok accounts after explicit authorization.

About Outpost

Outpost is designed for creators who want a simple and secure way to manage and publish their video content directly to their own TikTok accounts.

How It Works

  1. User logs in with TikTok via OAuth authentication
  2. User authorizes Outpost to access their account
  3. User uploads or prepares content
  4. User initiates publishing to their own TikTok account

Features

  • Secure TikTok login (OAuth)
  • Video upload and management
  • Direct publishing to user's own TikTok account

User Authorization

Outpost only performs actions explicitly requested and authorized by the user.

Compliance

Outpost does not support spam, bulk posting, or unauthorized posting. All publishing actions require explicit user interaction.

Who It Is For

Outpost is intended for individuals and creators who want to manage and publish their own content to TikTok in a controlled and authorized way.
