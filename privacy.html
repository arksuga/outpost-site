Privacy Policy for Outpost

Effective date: April 7, 2026

Outpost ("Outpost", "we", "our", or "us") provides a service that allows users to connect their own TikTok accounts and manage or publish content with their authorization.

Information We Collect

When you use Outpost, we may collect and process the following information:

  • TikTok account information made available through TikTok APIs, such as open ID, display name, avatar, and other basic profile information you authorize.
  • Access tokens and related authorization data required to keep your account connected and provide the service.
  • Content information that you choose to upload, prepare, or publish through Outpost.
  • Basic technical information required to operate the service, such as browser, device, IP address, and log data.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • authenticate your account and provide login functionality;
  • allow you to connect your TikTok account to Outpost;
  • enable you to upload, schedule, draft, or publish content to your own TikTok account when authorized by you;
  • maintain, secure, troubleshoot, and improve Outpost;
  • comply with legal obligations and enforce our policies.

How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal information.

We do not rent your personal information.

We do not share your personal information with third parties except when necessary to operate Outpost, comply with the law, protect our rights, or complete actions you request using connected platform services such as TikTok.

User Authorization

Outpost only performs actions on a user's TikTok account after the user has explicitly authorized the connection and requested the relevant action.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to provide the service, maintain security, comply with legal obligations, and resolve disputes.

Security

We take reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect information handled by Outpost. However, no method of storage or transmission is completely secure.

Your Rights

You may stop using Outpost at any time. You may also disconnect your linked account and request deletion of stored account-related information where applicable.

Children

Outpost is not intended for children under the age required by applicable law to use the service.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy for Outpost, please contact us through the contact method provided on our website.
